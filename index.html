This is an experiment I’ve developed for my 4th year honours project


Guide

  • Each Circle Represents an Actor in this environment.
    • Each one was created by a real person
    • The inner layer represents and actor’s personality or nature, they are decided by the person who created the Actor
    • The inner layer represents their collected experiences since you opened the webpage,
      • The larger the block of colour, the more time they’ve spent linked with that colour
      • An Actor’s experiences affect it’s willingness to associate with particular colours
      • it stagnates over time with weaker experiences disappearing
    • In the centre is a randomly generated glyph that is generated at the Actor’s inception, it’s purpose is only to differentiate it from other Actor’s so as to make them easier to follow and identify
    • After launching the website, a red circle is shown around the last Actor to be generated to make finding them easier
  • If two actors get close to each other, they become linked
    • The colour of the link depends on the sides of the Actors that are facing each other, as well as their willingness towards associating with the personality colour of the other Actor
    • if an Actor already has too much experience with a colour it will avoid it as best as it can
  • Each actor is assigned hidden behaviours at inception which affects its behaviour in very slight ways
